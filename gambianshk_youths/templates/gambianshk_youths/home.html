About Us

A vibrant and welcoming home away from home for Gambians living and working in this dynamic city of Hong Kong.

Our Mission

We are committed to building unity, fostering strength, and empowering every member of our community. Through cultural celebration, mutual support, and shared experiences, we strive to:

  • Connect Gambians across all generations and backgrounds in Hong Kong.
  • Preserve and promote Gambian culture, traditions, and values through engaging events and educational programs.
  • Provide practical support and resources to help our members thrive socially, professionally, and personally.
  • Advocate for the interests and welfare of Gambians living abroad.

What We Do

Our community will organize a wide range of activities, including:

  • Cultural festivals and national day celebrations that showcase the richness of Gambian heritage.
  • Workshops and seminars on topics such as career development, health awareness, and legal rights.
  • Social gatherings and networking events that build friendships and professional connections.
  • Support services for newcomers, students, and families adjusting to life in Hong Kong.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member?
You can register by clicking on the Register link and filling out the membership form or inform an admin.
Who can participate in community events?
All registered members and their families are welcome to participate in our events, friends and other interested individuals are welcome with request from admin.
How do I contribute to the community?
Contributions are managed by our admin team. Please contact us if you are interested in supporting our initiatives.
What is the main purpose of this Gambian Migrant Community App?
The app is designed to connect Gambians living in Hong Kong, providing resources, support, and a platform for sharing experiences and information.
Is the app free to use?
Yes, the app is completely free to download and use.
How can I connect with other members of the community?
You can connect with other members through the community forum, chat features, or by attending community events listed in the app.
How can I find out about community events?
You can find upcoming events in the "Events" section of the app or home page, contact admins for more details.
What types of resources are available in the app?
The app includes resources on employment, housing, legal rights, health services, and cultural activities relevant to the Gambian community.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All personal information is stored securely and is not shared with third parties without your consent.
How can I provide feedback about the app?
We welcome your feedback! You can submit your comments or suggestions through the "Feedback" section in the app.
What are the benefits of becoming a member?
Members gain access to exclusive events, resources, and a network of support from fellow Gambians in Hong Kong.
Are there any membership fees?
No, membership is free for all Gambians residing in Hong Kong.
Can I volunteer for community events?
Absolutely! We always welcome volunteers. Please reach out to us for more information on how to get involved.
How do you address community issues?
We hold regular meetings to discuss community concerns and work collaboratively to find solutions. Members are encouraged to voice their opinions.
Do you collaborate with other organizations?
Yes, we actively seek partnerships with other organizations to enhance our services and support for the community.
What should I do if I have a complaint?
Please contact our admin team directly via the contact form, and we will address your concerns promptly.
